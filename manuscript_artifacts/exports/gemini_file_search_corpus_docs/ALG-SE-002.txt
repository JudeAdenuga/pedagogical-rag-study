Title: Why 3x plus 5x is not 6x

Instructional content:
A common error is adding 3x and 5x incorrectly. The coefficients 3 and 5 tell how many x-groups there are. Three x-groups plus five x-groups gives eight x-groups, so 3x + 5x = 8x, not 6x.
