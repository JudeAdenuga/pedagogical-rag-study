Instructional content:
When variables appear on both sides, collect variable terms on one side and constants on the other while preserving equality. Example: 4n + 6 = 2n + 18. Subtract 2n from both sides to get 2n + 6 = 18. Then subtract 6 from both sides to get 2n = 12. Finally divide by 2, so n = 6.
