Instructional content:
When subtracting a grouped expression, the subtraction affects the entire group. In 6y - 2(3y - 5), the multiplier is -2, not just 2. Distribute -2 to both terms: -2 times 3y is -6y, and -2 times -5 is +10. The expression becomes 6y - 6y + 10, which simplifies to 10.
